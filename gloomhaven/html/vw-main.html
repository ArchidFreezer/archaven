Voidwarden

The Voidwarden is the support class of the Jaws of the Lion characters. She specializes in making other pathetic creatures perform her attacks for her or move as she sees fit. She has the largest hand size of the Jaws characters and some impressive losses to go with it. She also brings some welcome healing and some great crowd control. As a support character, she gets better the larger the party. She also really wants a melee ally (Red Guard being the better option of the two) to make one of her core cards work and thus she'll typically have a bad time if she's in a party with just a Hatchet.

Before going any further, I should talk about elements on this class in general. Ice is mostly useless, its use tied almost exclusively to losses, so you'll either use it for Master Influence or mostly not care about it except for when you're lining up a relevant loss. Dark is used by a number of abilities but is usually just a small bonus that's not essential and rarely worth playing around - the main exception to this being Lure of the Void top, where Dark can make a huge difference, but that's obviously situational. Accordingly, we like to have Dark around (both for the small bonuses and for Master Influence), and creating Dark is a welcome bonus, but it's also not really a driving force for our abilities or ability ordering.
