Elementalist

The Elementalist is my favourite class in Gloomhaven, mostly because it has some of the coolest flavour in the game. You start with mastery over fire, ice, wind, and earth, but your scholarly nature forces you to keep learning. As you level up, you slowly master light and dark in order to become the true elemental master!

The following are general observations of the class:

  • This class is all about elements, which means you always need to plan at least one turn ahead.
  • Mobility is bad. We are often using move 2s every turn, particularly at low levels. If we need to move more we are sacrificing our effectiveness. Bring items or allies that can help you move if possible.
  • Our Initiatives are pretty bad overall, especially at low levels. We have two good fast initiatives and two average fast initiatives, but we do not have access to ANY good slow initiatives. The slowest we can go is 67 initially.
  • Generate as much xp as possible at low levels because lvl 1 is pretty rough on this class.
