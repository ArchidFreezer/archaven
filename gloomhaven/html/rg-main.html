Red Guard

The Red Guard is a melee bruiser (hybrid between tank and damage dealer) class, the Jaw of the Lion equivalent to the Brute. Unlike the Brute, who turns out a bit underwhelming in Gloomhaven, the Red Guard is an amazing class in terms of design and balance. Basically every card this class has is good and most choices you make both while levelling up and deciding which cards to bring to a scenario will be difficult. It's hard to go wrong with the Red Guard and all of the best Jaws of the Lion party compositions include one.
