Geminate

The Geminate is a class that has two distinct hands: melee and ranged, only one of which may be in play at any one time. This means that if you need to lose a card to avoid damage you can only lose a card from the hand that is in play at the time. There is however a single discard pile so resting brings back cards from both hands.

Given the nature of the cards and the variance in their actions there is no single deck that will be used at each level. The deck needs to be tailored for each scenario, this is common for several classes but particularly pronounced for the Geminate. There are some basic principles that should be followed

  • You always need to have 7 cards in each hand at the start of a scenario
  • You should have at least a couple of form switching actions in each hand
  • On levelling up try and balance the cards you take so you don't become too lopsided
  • You have a very large hand size so burning cards is expected and most xp is attached to burns so don't horde these cards

In general the two halves of your nature have the following characteristics over and above the shared damage/movement actions

  • Melee - Uses Ice & Light
    Has tanking actions
  • Ranged - Uses Fire & Light
    Has support actions
