Hatchet

The Hatchet is a ranged damage-dealer who functions primarily around the core mechanic of his persistent loss card 'The Favorite.' He provides excellent and very consistent ranged damage with little downside. He is also one of the best classes at Looting in Gloomhaven and will regularly accrue large stacks of coins. The weaknesses of the class are that he mostly lacks crowd control, he has somewhat limited movement (and while this is mostly a recurring trend with the Jaws characters as Jaws scenarios were smaller on average, his is still quite poor in comparison), and he has average initiative. Still, he's very straightforward to play while also being very effective, making him a great beginner class who can still be good in any party.
