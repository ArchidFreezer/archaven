Brute

The Brute is a melee bruiser (hybrid between tank and damage dealer) class.

The Brute does have some slightly more complicated and complex combos at high Prosperity, most of the time this is simply the more straightforward class in Gloomhaven, focusing most of its turns around moving and attacking.

While definitely not a true 'tank' in the sense that it can just wade into the fray and absorb hits left and right, it does have some tankiness and can take some number of hits when your party otherwise has no way to avoid them.
