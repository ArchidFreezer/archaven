Berserker

The Berserker, a tanky melee damage-dealer class that has unique life-based mechanics.

She is difficult to level up because of the following:

  • Her level 1 cards are already really strong, so it sucks to replace them.
  • Most of the cards she can choose from are really strong.
  • Most of her cards have a lot of synergy, so removing a single card feels like you could be dismantling a machine in many cases.
