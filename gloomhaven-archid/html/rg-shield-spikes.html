Red Guard Shield Spikes

This Red Guard build centres around the Shield Spikes card that retaliates damage based on the Shield value at the time of the hit. This means that the build is not overly interested in the AMD or damage abilities, focussing instead on consistently increasing our shield value. We use both enhancements and items to increase this value. Because some of the decenbt shileding items are not found until higher wealth levels the build can feel a little underwhelming at the start if these have not been unlocked yet.

Applying the Immobilize status effect is also very useful to us as it means we can lock down ranged enemies so they do not attempt to move away and we can get our retaliate to proc.

So why would you play a Shield Spikes Build?

  • Strong against the typically difficult high shield/retaliate enemies.
  • Strong against swarming enemies as they tend to be low health, low damage and our shields can negate much if not all the damage, whilst dishing out direct damage back.

The Shield Spikes build loves the following:

  • Party members who can provide us with Shield or Immobilize enemies. There is a great synergy with the Sawbones persistent loss that gives a permanent shield 1, and even better with the upgraded version that allows us to ignore negative status effects. The Spellweaver, Tinkerer and Voidwarden also have cards that can help.
  • Party members that can heal us, especially at lower levels when we have loiwer shield values.
  • Unusually we want to be hit a lot so Stun is not something we want on adjacent enemies, we are happy to have them draw a Curse as that means they still attack.
