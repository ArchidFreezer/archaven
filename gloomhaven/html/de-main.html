Demolitionist

The Demo is a mid-health, 9 card class that functions as the 'melee dps' of the JotL crew. This puts them in the same category as base Gloom’s Scoundrel. At higher levels (6+), when the Demo's perk deck has become the best in the game among classes that actually attack (basically guaranteed +2), they can compare reasonably well. At lower levels, they suffer from initiatives that just aren't fast enough to allow you to really hit and run, and thus become significantly riskier to play with arguably less reward. Lastly, they will always suffer from a crippling lack of any healing whatsoever. Now, healing is generally a losing battle in Gloomhaven, but some healing is always needed in order to clear off poison and wound (especially against JotL's Zealots) or to make sure you aren’t at 1 health for half the game.

They also have an obstacle destruction and 'pin against walls' subtheme, which, while situational, are much less situational than people seem to think, even in base Gloom (there isn’t any issue in JotL, where the maps are balanced to help the Demo in this area). On an average base Gloom 3 room scenario, you’ll be able to destroy about 4 obstacles for useful benefit, which comes out to about 1 destruction per rest cycle. This is something to keep in mind when evaluating your choices of obstacle destroyers. Obviously, this means you get insane extra value from a Cragheart teammate, who can place obstacles for your benefit.
