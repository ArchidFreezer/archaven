Drifter

The Drifter has a 12 card hand and a high (10hp at level 1) health pool, which allows the player to spend some of their resources without worrying about shortening their stamina or effective 'lifespan' too much. The Drifter is based around the concept of versatility, sporting a single main mechanic and utilizing no elements whatsoever.

The Drifter's only mechanic is the 'use slots', or persistent abilities. The best part is that all of the core cards to change playstyles are found at Level 1/X, which means that you are flexible even between scenarios. Given the hand size you can expect to be able to two at a minimum of often 3 of these cards.
