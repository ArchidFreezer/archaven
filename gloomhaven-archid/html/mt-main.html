Mindthief

Okay, time for the Mindthief, my personal favourite starting class. Personal favourite to play, that is, certainly not the design. What's wrong with the design? Well, the class appears to be designed such that you're supposed to switch around Augments depending on the circumstances but in reality, you should pretty much just play The Mind's Weakness nearly 100% of the time (especially pre-9).

So what makes this class so fun? High power level with high difficulty to execute properly. The Mindthief has quite possibly the highest consistent damage of any of the starting classes combined with certainly the best crowd control at level 1. She also has great mobility and amazing initiatives. What's the catch? Well, she's a mostly-melee character, especially at low level, with a very small hp pool.
